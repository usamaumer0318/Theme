FAQ

How do I add my existing tool code?

Open this file, find the <!-- TOOLS GRID --> section, and replace a card's inner HTML with your tool's form and JavaScript. Keep the .card class for styling.

Does it work on phones?

Yes. The layout is mobile‑first and adapts to tablets and desktops with a responsive CSS grid.

Can I switch to light mode?

Use the floating moon/sun button at the bottom‑right. Your choice is saved for next time.
